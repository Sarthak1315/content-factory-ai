This report fact-checks claims made in the article 'The Great SMB Culling: AI Isn't an Upgrade, It's Your Survival Kit for 2025'. Several claims regarding AI's impact on operational costs, specific adoption rates, and future predictions are assessed. While the general sentiment about AI's growing importance for SMBs is widely supported, specific statistics and events mentioned in the article are largely unverified or lack direct corroboration from credible sources. The article makes strong, often hyperbolic, statements that are difficult to fact-check with precise data.
